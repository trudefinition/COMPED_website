About Me

Anton Amoncio is a student of University of the Philippines Open University. He is currently taking up Bachelor of Arts in Multimedia Studies. He works as a Marketing Consultant and a Social Media Specialist. He does video shoots and photoshoots depending on his clients' request. His contract usually lasts for at least six months to a year depending on his clients' marketing campaigns. Most of his work can be found online since they are used for marketing purposes on social media sites like Facebook and Instagram.

He worked with the supplement brand "ProMatrix7" and helped them set-up their social media sites and was able to gather celebrities and influencers to endorse their products. After that, he worked with New Creation Manufacturing that specializes in baby clothes. He helped in revamping their social media presence through Facebook and Instagram. He also worked as their Marketing Consultant and helped them with useful marketing campaigns and pegs in order to strengthen their brand.

After his work was completed, he moved on to Infinity Liquid Glass Shield Trading Corp. that sold water and dirt repellant sprays called "Nanofied". He worked as their marketing consultant, videographer and social media specialist and helped them work from the ground up since the product was new and was the first of its kind in the country. The owner of that company and Anton's bond grew stronger and he has now become his partner on a new business venture that specializes in supplements. XLR8 Biotech was born!

Anton is now the Marketing Manager of XLR8 Biotech that produces Selective Androgen Receptor Modulators or also know as SARMs. These supplements are for athletes, aspirants and those who commit to be fit. It's been running successfully now for almost two years and have gathered thousands of loyal customers all over the Philippines. Though this is not the first of its kind in the Philippines, it's the only one that is still running because of how they have established good relationships with their clients.

He dreams of becoming a Commercial Pilot in the near future and he knows that for him to get there, he must work on his bachelors degree. He chose University of the Philippines Open University because they offer distance education. This enables him to work and at the same time study.

On his spare time, Anton would stay home and watch movies or play mobile games.
